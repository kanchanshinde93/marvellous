KYC, or Know Your Customer, is a regulatory requirement designed to prevent fraud and money laundering. As part of the KYC process, investors are required to provide identifying information and documentation. This helps ensure the legitimacy of investment transactions and protects both investors and financial institutions.

Completing your KYC and providing bank details is a straightforward process. Most investment firms have online portals where you can upload the necessary documents securely. Once verified, your bank account details will be linked to your investment account for seamless transactions.

Missing the deadline for KYC submission may result in delays in processing your investment transactions. It's essential to adhere to the KYC requirements and submit the necessary documents promptly to avoid any inconvenience.
